Hello and welcome to my home page, internet stranger!

I am Devang. I am a PhD student in Astrophysics at Newcastle University in the UK. I study the evolution of supermassive black holes over billions of years, using some of the coolest (quite literally!) telescopes in the world.

I spend my free time either thinking about fun project ideas or cursing myself for not working on those fun project ideas. Though I do occasionally dabble into some of them. This website (will) house(s) exciting (or mundane!) things in my life.

If you are looking to learn more about my research (or offering me a job) then go to the research page. Navigate to Science Communication page if you are looking for fun science interactives, quizzes, or list of outreach activities I have done in the past. Blog has (well, blogs!) my thoughts on random topics. However, be careful if you decide to visit Clicky Stuff page, it has links to some of the unarguably best content on the internet which may lead to hours of wasted time. If none of that takes your fancy, Fun cool things page might be the one for you!
